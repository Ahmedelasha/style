Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I exercise?
A: It's recommended to exercise at least 3-4 times a week for optimal health benefits.
Q: What are the best foods for a healthy diet?
A: A balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains is best for maintaining good health.
Q: How can I improve my mental health?
A: Regular exercise, a healthy diet, adequate sleep, and mindfulness practices can all contribute to better mental health.
Q: What should I eat before a workout?
A: A small meal or snack with carbohydrates and protein, such as a banana with peanut butter, is ideal before a workout.
